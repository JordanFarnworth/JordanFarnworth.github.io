Frequently Asked Questions

  • Is ultrasound safe for my baby?

    Yes, it is very safe! There is no radiation involved with ultrasound. We follow ALARA guidelines to keep scan times short to ensure the saftey of the baby.

  • What if I can't see the gender?

    Sometimes depending on the position of the baby, we are unable to see the gender of the baby. If this occurs we will redo the ultrasound at no cost to you at a later time.

  • Do I still need to get an ultrasound at my doctor?

    YES! We provide non-diagnostic ultrasounds to give you a chance to see your baby before they are born. Your care provider will order ultrasounds to check the anatomy of your baby. We will not scan unless you are up to date with visits to your care provider.

  • Do you provide images and video after the visit?

    Yes, depending on your package you will receive either a digital or hard copy of the images that our technologist records during your visit.

  • Can I have a 3D image done on a mobile visit?

    No, currently we only offer 2D images on mobile visits due to the lack of funciontality in our mobile friendly ultrascan machines.
